# Welcome to Howard Gradbot! 🎓

Hey there, Howard University students! Welcome to Howard Gradbot, your ultimate graduation planning assistant designed to make your academic journey smoother and more efficient. Whether you're a freshman just starting out or a senior fine-tuning your graduation checklist, Gradbot is here to help you every step of the way.

## Problem Statement 🎯

Many students at Howard University face challenges when planning their graduation due to outdated course information and complex requirements. This often leads to a prolonged and stressful academic journey, with students signing up for the wrong classes or missing available ones, ultimately delaying their graduation.

## Product Overview 🚀

Howard Gradbot is a Google Sheets plugin powered by ChatGPT, offering up-to-date course data and personalized guidance. By integrating directly with Google Sheets, a platform familiar to many students, Gradbot aims to simplify graduation planning and enhance the overall academic experience at Howard University.

## Goals 🎯

- **Streamlined Planning:** Simplify the graduation checklist planning process with a user-friendly interface.
- **Accurate Course Information:** Provide the most up-to-date course descriptions, credit hours, and major requirements.
- **Enhanced Student Experience:** Offer personalized recommendations and guidance tailored to each student's needs.

## Completion Criteria ✔️

- **Functional Chatbot Interface:** Seamlessly integrated within Google Sheets for easy access.
- **Course Information Retrieval:** Pulls relevant data from the university website for accurate guidance.
- **Personalized Recommendations:** Offers tailored suggestions based on user inputs.
- **Error Handling:** Effectively handles common user queries with clear feedback.
- **User Satisfaction:** Gathers feedback to continuously improve the user experience.
